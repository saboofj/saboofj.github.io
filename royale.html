ROYALE

TIMELESS CLASSIC

Inspired by affluent life styles of the Era of Royals, the pieces of Royale Collectionare iconic representations of their history which were adorned by precious and rare jewels.

A classic composition to create heirloom pieces, Royale features rare precious gemstones enhanced by fusion cut diamonds, a new creation of the finest brilliant-cut diamonds.

Rare and certified centre-stones play protagonist, set in gold or platinum. Royale represents a timeless pieces of wearable art, to be passed from generations to generations.
