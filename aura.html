AURA

INLAY LUXURY

It is believed that all objects & living things manifest an Aura. It is this very essence that is captured by Saboo in this collection. Inspired by the halo of divine souls, the pieces of the collection engage exceptional rare gemstones as the centre, which are inlayed in natural gems stones, embracing the main stone and defining its Aura.

Saboo’s Aura collection uses Inlay & engraving techniques which are visible in the majestic Taj Mahal,the epitome of Mughal architecture. The most striking feature of the Mughal architecture is engraving of marble to embrace special gems stones.

From the very beginning, Saboo Fine Jewels have continued to create ‘magic’, with true luxury. The Aura jewels integrate exquisite craftsmanship, refined details, intrinsic designs and magnificent gemstones.

The pieces hide a strong and fascinating structure resulting in a collection with a bold character and an intense story to tell.
