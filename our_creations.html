Our Creations

 

Royale

TIMELESS CLASSIC

Inspired by the affluent life styles of the Era of Royals, the pieces of Royale Collections are iconic representations of their history which was adorned by timeless precious high jewels.

A classic composition of treasured jewels, Royale features timeless composition of rare precious gemstones enhanced by the fusion cut diamonds a new creation of the finest brilliant-cut in diamonds, in harmony to create timeless heirloom pieces.

The rare precious natural gemstones are key ingredient of Saboo High Jewels Universe which is fabricated into beautiful design.

Rare and certified centre-stones play protagonist, set in gold or platinum. Royale represents timeless pieces of wearable art, to be passed from generation to generation.

AURA

INLAY LUXURY

It is believed that all objects & living things manifest an Aura. It is this very essence that is captured by Saboo in Aura. Inspired by the halo of divine souls, and the aura emitted around them, the pieces of the Aura collection engage exceptional rare gemstones as the center, which are inlayed in natural gems stones, embracing the main stone and defining its Aura.

Saboo’s Aura collection uses Inlay & engraving techniques which are visible in the majestic Taj Mahal, situated in India’s ancient city of Agra. The most striking feature of the Mugal architect in the famed monument is engraving of marble to embrace special gems stones.

To carve the surrounding precious stones involves skillful hands and a deep knowledge of stone-cutting. A task which Saboo’s adept craftsmen handle with perfection.

The detailed and refined designs hide a strong and fascinating structure resulting in a collection with a bold character and an intense story to tell.

The Aura jewels integrate exquisite craftsmanship, refined details, intrinsic designs and magnificent gemstones. From its beginnings, Saboo Fine Jewels have continued to create ‘magic’, with true luxury.

 

DEW DROP

REIGNING DIAMONDS

Rare gemstones and diamonds are handpicked for this collection with the one and only criteria of being "exclusive". These miracles of nature are so coveted by connoisseurs who 'hunt' for exceptional gemstones.

The jewels in the Dew Drops collection are designed with the centre stone playing protagonist and to enhance its brightness and dazzling beauty, it is surrounded by dangling rose-cut diamonds & briolette diamonds.

These rose-cut briolette give an illusion of floating diamonds, designed like an echo of magical décor surrounding the centre-stone.

Diamonds rain upon the wearable art of Dew Drops.

The rose-cut & briolette diamonds are drilled in an exclusive symmetrical manner and are connected to the main body of the piece with fine gold wire.

A pure technical craftsmanship suspended with no visibility of metal.

ELEMENTO

VOLUMES IN TITANIUM

In the Saboo universe, the art of jewellery making is taken to another dimension, as titanium premiers in the Elemento pieces.

With its exquisite combination the pieces in the Elemento collection take on three-dimensional shapes showing charm, femininity and boldness.

Known to be the toughest amongst metal, titanium is a space age material, used in spaceships. Additionally, it has an amazing range of colour and unique sheen properties which produce a spectacular effect.

As per desire, titanium could be violet, red, blue, pink and various colour hues, which is then often combined with hand-picked gemstones to give balance to each piece, and further accentuated with diamonds.

In the Elemento collection, Saboo introduces a revolutionary metal – titanium, for a bold statement adhering to women who have a flair for big & bold pieces with desirable comfort.

Only a numbered jewellery globally produce titanium jewellery, as it’s a highly technical art requiring a tremendous amount of skill and working hours.

The Elemento collection inspires an irresistible urge to adorn the arms, neck, ears and fingers with the most exquisite jewels in the world.

 

Yaksha kuber

MAHARANI’S JEWELS

From monuments and ancient paintings to modern interpretations, the most beautiful icons have been adorned with gorgeous jewels, be it due to their symbols of power, beauty or then seduction

Saboo is inspired by these historical reminders in the Yaksh Kuber collection where a ‘reincarnation’ of the royal jewels from the Mughal period is unveiled.

A re-birth of a speciality master craft after it got lost amongst the popularity of various other modern techniques of jewellery-making, the pieces of Yaksh Kuber create ‘magic’, fit for the modern Diva.

Ethnic Indian-style jewels are made popular by Saboo, showcasing its expertise in the art of ‘Inlay’ and ‘Partaash’, a decorative technique involving the highest excellence and precision of inserting precious gemstones and engraving into a sculpted gold base, to form patterns and pictures that are seamlessly set within the warmth of the noble metal.

Yash Kuber is the Maharaja’s gift of jewels for today’s Maharani.

Taj Partash

ANCIENT CRAFT

The combination of marble engravings together with inlay-work found in the Taj Mahal, is inspiration for Taj Partash collection.

Seeped in tradition and invented by the Mughals, the work of Partash originated more than 500 years ago. But, during the British rule in India, which followed the Mughal period, this workmanship somewhat disappeared.

In the Taj Partash collection, Saboo has revived this ancient art.

Here diamond and gemstones are set b Kundan setting method i.e. setting in 24-carat gold, while the jewels are made in 22-carat gold. Essentially, Taj Partash is a combination of three skills - Engraving, Kundan setting and Inlay. The complete fabrication process is done by hand tools…. Similar to what was used 500 years ago with little enhancement.

Entirely, hand-crafted, the pieces in the Taj Partash collection are most labour intensive taking many months to complete just one piece and thus, the pieces are highly sought after.

 

Kaustubh

PEARL MAGIC

Saboo brings spectacular and innovative jewellery to markets around the globe, right from the source. In the Kaustubh collection, each piece is centred around hand-selected, magnificent natural pearls.

The pearls are so meticulously weaved to the base of the jewel, as if they were never separated, with a beautiful enamel on its inner side. Interesting design elements and stones are used to further enhance its beauty. The Kaustubh collection draws inspiration from the formation of a pearl within an oyster, and the jewels’ painstakingly-crafted woven-pearls exterior reveals an exceptional know-how of this unique craft.

This collection exemplifies the signature art of pearl-weaving which Saboo is known for, harmoniously blending gemstones and yellow gold to highlight the natural golden lustre of the pearls.
